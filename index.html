Crafting & Smelting Recipes

Crafting Table

Recipe: 4 Wood Planks (any type)

Required for most crafting.

Layout: 2x2 grid of planks.

Furnace

Recipe: 8 Cobblestone

Used for smelting ores, cooking food, and making glass.

Layout: Cobblestone in all slots except the center.

Iron Pickaxe

Recipe: 3 Iron Ingots, 2 Sticks

Mines stone, iron, coal, and other basic ores.

Layout: 3 Ingots top row, 1 Stick middle-center, 1 Stick bottom-center.

Glass

Smelting: Sand + Fuel

Used for windows and decorative blocks.

Mob Information & Drops

Zombie

Health: 20 (10 hearts)

Attack: 2-3 hearts (Easy-Hard)

Drops: Rotten Flesh (0-2), Iron Ingot (rare), Carrot (rare), Potato (rare).

Creeper

Health: 20 (10 hearts)

Attack: Explodes (variable damage)

Drops: Gunpowder (0-2). Music Disc (if killed by Skeleton).

Cow

Health: 10 (5 hearts)

Attack: None

Drops: Leather (0-2), Raw Beef (1-3).

Resource Locations

Iron Ore

Location: Common in most biomes, layers 0-64.

Usage: Iron tools, armor, buckets, shears.

Diamonds

Location: Rare, layers 0-16 (most common at Y=11-12).

Usage: Diamond tools, armor, enchantment table.

Oak Wood

Location: Oak Forests, Plains, Rivers.

Usage: Basic building, crafting material.

Netherite Scraps

Location: Ancient Debris in the Nether, layers 8-22 (most common at Y=15).

Usage: Upgrading Diamond gear to Netherite.
